About Grand Slam Surfacing

Discover our journey, values, and the passionate team behind our exceptional resurfacing and construction services.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the resurfacing and construction industry, Grand Slam Surfacing has grown from a small local business to a trusted name across Eastern Cape, Western Cape, and Free State.

With over a decade of experience, we've built our reputation on delivering exceptional quality, innovative solutions, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, learning, and a passion for excellence.

Today, we're proud to have a team of 11-25 skilled professionals who bring unparalleled expertise to every project, ensuring that each client receives the premium service they deserve.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To transform spaces and enhance communities through exceptional resurfacing and construction services that stand the test of time.
