What We Do

Detailed explanations of our core services and how we deliver exceptional results.

Resurfacing Services

Our resurfacing services breathe new life into worn-out surfaces, providing a fresh, durable finish that enhances both aesthetics and functionality. Whether it's tennis courts, netball courts, driveways, or pathways, we use premium materials and advanced techniques to ensure long-lasting results.

We begin with thorough surface preparation, removing loose materials and repairing any damage. Our expert team then applies high-quality coatings in multiple layers, ensuring even coverage and optimal adhesion. The result is a smooth, professional finish that meets industry standards and exceeds client expectations.

Key Features:

  • Premium material selection
  • Expert surface preparation
  • Durable, long-lasting finish
  • Professional line marking

Construction Services

Our construction services cover the complete building process from initial planning to final execution. We specialize in creating new sports facilities, including tennis courts, netball courts, and multi-purpose recreational areas. Our team handles all aspects of construction, ensuring compliance with industry standards and safety regulations.

We start with comprehensive site analysis and preparation, including excavation and drainage solutions. Our skilled craftsmen then construct solid foundations using high-quality concrete and materials. Throughout the project, we maintain strict quality control measures and keep clients informed of progress.

Key Features:

  • Complete project management
  • Site preparation and excavation
  • High-quality concrete work
  • Compliance with safety standards

Sports Equipment Installation

We provide professional installation of all types of sports equipment, specializing in netball poles and tennis court accessories. Our team ensures precise placement and secure installation that meets official regulations and safety requirements.

Using high-quality materials and advanced installation techniques, we guarantee equipment that will withstand years of use while maintaining optimal performance. Our installations include proper anchoring systems, safety padding, and finishing touches that enhance the overall appearance of your facility.

Key Features:

  • Regulation-compliant installation
  • Precision placement and leveling
  • Durable anchoring systems
  • Safety padding and finishing
